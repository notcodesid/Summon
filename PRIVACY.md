# Summon Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Summon is an early Devnet collectible game. This policy explains what information is used when you sign in and make a pull.

## Information used by Summon

- **Account information:** When you continue with Google, Privy processes the identity information needed to sign you in. Summon may display the email address returned with your account.
- **Wallet information:** Privy creates and operates an embedded Solana wallet for your account. Public wallet addresses and transactions are visible on Solana and cannot be made private or deleted from the blockchain.
- **Game information:** Your player account, pull history, inventory, randomness result, and settlement data are stored on public blockchain infrastructure.
- **Technical information:** Privy, Solana RPC providers, MagicBlock, Google, and hosting providers may process device, network, diagnostic, or usage information needed to provide their services.

Summon does not ask for or store your Google password, wallet private key, or seed phrase. Never send those credentials to anyone claiming to provide Summon support.

## How information is used

Information is used to authenticate you, create and operate your embedded wallet, submit and verify collectible pulls, display your collection, prevent abuse, diagnose failures, and maintain the app.

Summon does not sell personal information.

## Service providers

Summon relies on third-party services, including Privy for authentication and embedded wallets, Google for sign-in, Solana RPC infrastructure, and MagicBlock for Ephemeral Rollups and verifiable randomness. Their own policies apply to information they process.

## Retention and deletion

Public blockchain data is permanent and cannot be erased by Summon. Deleting a Summon login account does not delete its public wallet address, transactions, player account, or inventory from Solana.

Privy account deletion is destructive. It disassociates the login account from its embedded wallet, and signing back in later may create a different wallet. Users should move any assets they control before requesting deletion. The Account screen provides a deletion-request action that opens the configured Summon support channel.

## Children

Summon is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Do not use the app if you are below the minimum age required to consent to data processing in your country.

## Changes

This policy may change as Summon develops. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

## Contact

Use **Request account deletion** in the app's Account screen for privacy questions and deletion requests. The public support address configured for the release must also be shown on the Summon website and store listing.

# Summon Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Summon is an early mobile app for a real-world animal collection experience on Solana. This policy will be updated as sign-in, camera, location, and onchain features ship.

## Current shell

The initial app shell does not collect personal account data on its own. As features are added (authentication, wallets, camera/scanning, network requests), this policy will describe:

- **Account information** used to sign in
- **Wallet information** (public addresses and onchain activity)
- **Game information** stored on public blockchain infrastructure
- **Device / camera / location** data if used for scanning and exploration
- **Technical information** from service providers needed to run the app

Summon will not ask for or store wallet private keys or seed phrases. Never send those credentials to anyone claiming to provide Summon support.

## Contact

Support contact will be published with store listing and app configuration when the product is ready for release.
